# 🍒 Bloomly (working name)

A gentle productivity app to manage **habits + tasks** in one calm, beautiful space.

Built with 💗 using **Vue + Ionic**, designed to help you make progress without pressure.

---

## ✨ Why Bloomly?

Most productivity apps feel loud, stressful, or overwhelming.  
Bloomly is different.

- 🌱 Focus on *gentle progress*, not perfection  
- 🫧 Clean, soft, calming design  
- ✅ Tasks **and** habits in one place  
- 📱 Works offline & can be installed on your phone (PWA)

Small steps. Tiny wins. Real consistency.

---

## 🧸 Features

- ✔️ Create & complete daily tasks  
- 🔁 Build habits with simple tracking  
- 📆 See what you need to do *today*  
- 🌸 Minimal, cute, and distraction-free UI  
- 📱 Installable on iPhone via “Add to Home Screen”

> No accounts. No pressure. Just you and your rhythm.

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

- Soft colors & lots of white space  
- Rounded UI, gentle animations  
- Encouraging instead of demanding  
- “Business-quality, but kind”

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Vue 3**
- **Vite**
- **Ionic Framework**
- Local storage (offline-first)
- PWA-ready
